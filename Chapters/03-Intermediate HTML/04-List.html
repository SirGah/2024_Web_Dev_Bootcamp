Recepe for Pinoy Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cooked Rice
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Gound Pepper
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • Cooking Oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Onion String
  • Ommelette:
    • 3 Eggs
    • Chopped Onions
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Sugar

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat your pan in medium heat
  2. Add oil fry the choppped garlic until brown
  3. Add the cooked rice
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  5. Add 2 beaten eggs in the pan, mix well with the rice
  6. Stir & mix the pan for 2 mins
  7. Placed the fried rice to a clean plate
  8. Add thinly sliced string onions on top and serve
